Who We Are

About BORA Foundation

Transforming lives through compassion and sustainable community development

Making a Difference

Creating Lasting Change in Communities

BORA Foundation is dedicated to empowering underserved communities through innovative programs in education, healthcare, and sustainable development. We believe that every individual deserves access to opportunities that enable them to reach their full potential.

Our Mission

Creating lasting change through education, healthcare, and sustainable development initiatives.

Our Vision

A world where all communities have resources and support to thrive and reach their potential.

Our Approach

Collaborative partnerships and grassroots initiatives guided by transparency and dignity.

What We Do

Our Programs

Three core pillars creating meaningful and sustainable impact in communities

01

Education

Laying the foundation for vulnerable children's futures by investing in their education. We pay school fees, provide uniforms, and cover all school requirements, helping children return to school and chase their dreams.

  • School Fees Payment
  • School Uniforms
  • School Supplies
  • Educational Support
Support Education
Most Needed
02

Healthcare

Supporting vulnerable families by providing medical insurance (Mutuelle de Santé). At just 5 Euros per person annually, we make healthcare accessible to families who cannot afford it, ensuring preventive care and treatment.

  • Medical Insurance (Mutuelle)
  • Family Coverage
  • Healthcare Access
  • Preventive Care
Support Healthcare
03

Self-Sufficiency

Empowering vulnerable people—widows, widowers, single mothers, and orphans—by providing sewing machines, starting capital, and income-generating resources, enabling them to earn a sustainable living.

  • Sewing Machines
  • Starting Capital
  • Income Generation
  • Skills Empowerment
Support Self-Sufficiency
